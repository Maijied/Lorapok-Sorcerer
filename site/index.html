05 / FIELD NOTES

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to move from curious to enchanted.

Does Sorcerer upload my media anywhere else?

No. Sorcerer sends directly from your browser to the Discord webhook you configure. There is no Lorapok backend or tracking layer.

What happens when the original cannot be found?

The engine keeps the strongest page candidates, verifies media type and size, and offers a URL fallback when an attachment is unavailable.

Which browsers are supported?

Firefox uses MV2. Chrome, Edge, Opera, and Brave use the shared Chromium MV3 build.
